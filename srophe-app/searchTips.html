Search Tips

Search results can be improved by the use of the following search operators:

Wildcards

? single character wildcard and * multiple characters

ed?ssa
aba*

Phrase searches

"Enclose terms in double quotes"

"aba catholicos"

Proximity operators

~n search for two or more words that occur within a specified number of words. 

aba catholicos~2

Fuzzy searching

~ searches close matches.

aba~
